Divyam Ramji Mehrotra

Divyam Ramji Mehrotra, a full-time wedding photographer for the past 7 years, has covered 120+ weddings as the main photographer. When it comes to professional degrees, Divyam has completed a Diploma in Visual Effects. He is the founder of Weddingrams, a full-time candid and destination wedding photography boutique, based in Gurugram, India. He is the co-founder of Photopedia. A Fujifilm mentor, photography is everything for him. His work has been featured in PopXO, Wedmegood, Hindustan Times, Brides Today, and many other places. His services include pre/post wedding shoots, cinematographed wedding videos, and wedding day tales. Divyam was awarded the ‘wedding photographer of the year 2018-2019’ by Sony Better photography awards, the ‘Best Photographer, India 2018’ by One Eyeland, and various category awards at Weddingsutra 2018. Talking about Divyam’s style, it depicts real people, real moments in a creative way and not just by a template.
